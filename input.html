What is a headless environment?
A headless server is a computing device without a local interface that is dedicated to providing services to other computers and their users. Headless, in this context, basically means that the computing device has no monitor or peripherals, such as a keyboard and mouse.

Description

A puppeteer is a person who manipulates an inanimate object that might be shaped like a human, animal or mythical creature, or another object to create the illusion that the puppet is "alive". The puppeteer may be visible to or hidden from the audience. Wikipedia
